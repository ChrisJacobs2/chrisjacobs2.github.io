Tired of eating cafeteria slop? Want to try something new? Maybe something a little more local? Well, you're in luck! (unless your picky) This here blog post is dedicated to showing YOU some of the best places to eat near the UNCG Petty building. That said, don't sleep on the Cafe, they have great food given the price. These food still better.

Don

A "simple, booth-lined Japanese restaurant specializing in hot entrees like donburi, teriyaki & ramen." Don is a small Japanese restaurant located on Tate street. It's a great place to go if you're in the mood for ramen, udon, or soups. They also have rice bowls and other entrees. I have personally been there, and it has a great atmosphere. The food is also very good. I ate the udon, but I have been told the ramen is delicious as well.
